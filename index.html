Mission & Goals

The mission of RAILMAP is to provide a scalable, digital twin of the U.S. railway infrastructure to enhance safety, efficiency, and modernization.

System Architecture

The platform utilizes UAVs, terrestrial scanning (FARO), and a photorealistic 5D digital modeling system for national deployment.

Texas Pilot

The initial pilot is focused on the Dallas–Houston corridor, integrating with Class I freight operators and urban transit nodes.
